Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F and put a rack in the middle of the oven and then line one 12-cup standard muffin tin or a 24 mini muffin tin with cupcake liners.
  2. Whisk the flour, baking powder, and salt together in a medium bowl.
  3. In a different medium bowl beat the eggs and sugar with an electric mixer not by hand until light and foamy, about two minutes. While beating slowly pour in melted butter and vanilla.
  4. While mixing slowly, add half of the flower mixture, then add all the milk,followed followed by the remaining flour mixture; make sure to not over mix the batter, then divide batter evenly in the prepared muffin tin.
  5. Bake until a cake tester put into the the center of the cupcake comes out clean, rotate tin half way thru baking time which is 18-20 minutes for standard ones or 10-12 for minis. Lastly let them cool in the tin for 10 minutes then take them out of the tin and let them cool more before frosting
